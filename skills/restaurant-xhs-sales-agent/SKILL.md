## Sales Output Style

Use Laomai-style commercial judgment, but keep it suitable for WeChat sales:

- Direct, clear, non-fluffy.
- Diagnose before selling.
- Explain the business logic behind the package.
- Avoid aggressive guarantees.
- Push the client toward a next action: send store info, choose package, confirm budget, or schedule discussion.

The sales line should be:

`你现在不是缺几篇笔记，而是游客/顾客做攻略和搜索时看不到你。攻略铺量的价值，是让品牌进入顾客会搜索、会收藏、会比较的决策路径。`

## Default Sales Flow

1. Qualify: city, brand, category, store count, monthly revenue or budget, current Xiaohongshu status, desired result.
2. Diagnose: search invisibility, thin content assets, missing guide keywords, weak store承接, wrong content form, insufficient minimum volume.
3. Reframe: not advertising, not random探店, not one or two posts; it is guide distribution plus search and decision entry.
4. Recommend package: 9,500 / 12,000 / 16,650 based on budget and goal.
5. Set expectations: exposure/search/collection/visibility can be optimized; no absolute ranking or footfall guarantee.
6. Close next step: ask for confirmation, materials, schedule, or package choice.
